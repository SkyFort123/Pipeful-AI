Frequently Asked Questions

What counts as a conversation?

A conversation is one complete interview session where Pipeful AI calls you and updates one or more deals in your CRM.

Can I change plans later?

Yes! You can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. Changes take effect immediately.

Do you offer annual billing?

Yes, annual plans come with a 20% discount. Contact us for annual pricing.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards. Enterprise customers can also pay via invoice.

Is there a setup fee?

No setup fees for Free or Standard plans. Enterprise plans may include setup depending on custom requirements.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes, you can cancel your subscription at any time. No long-term contracts required.
