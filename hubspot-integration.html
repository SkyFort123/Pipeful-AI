HubSpot Integration

Connect Pipeful AI with your HubSpot account to update deals, log activities, and maintain a clean pipeline—all hands-free.

Why HubSpot + Pipeful AI?

HubSpot is your command center for inbound sales, but you shouldn't need to be at your computer to keep it updated. Pipeful AI integrates seamlessly with HubSpot, enabling you to manage deals, contacts, and activities through voice—whether you're commuting, walking, or multitasking.

Key Features

Deal Pipeline Management

Move deals through stages, update amounts, and set close dates—all through natural conversation.

Contact & Company Updates

Keep contact records and company information current with insights from your conversations.

Custom Properties

Pipeful adapts to your custom deal, contact, and company properties automatically.

Activity Tracking

Automatically log calls, emails, meetings, and notes with full context and timestamps.

Deal Stage Automation

Intelligently move deals through your pipeline based on conversation insights and methodology validation.

Secure OAuth Connection

Connect via HubSpot's OAuth 2.0. Your data stays secure and within your HubSpot account.

How It Works

Setting up Pipeful AI with HubSpot is quick and easy:

  1. Install the Pipeful AI Chrome extension
  2. Navigate to your HubSpot account and click "Connect HubSpot"
  3. Authorize Pipeful AI via HubSpot's secure OAuth flow
  4. Configure which properties and pipelines you want to sync
  5. Start adding deals to your interview queue

Supported HubSpot Objects

Pipeful AI works with all core HubSpot objects:

  • Deals (all pipelines)
  • Contacts
  • Companies
  • Activities (calls, emails, meetings, notes)
  • Custom objects (on request)
