Security & Privacy

Your CRM data is sensitive. We treat it that way. All data is encrypted, 100% owned by you, and never sold or mined. Learn how Pipeful AI protects your information with enterprise-grade security and private LLM deployment options.

Our Security Commitment

At Pipeful AI, security isn't an afterthought—it's built into every layer of our platform. We understand that your CRM contains your most valuable business data, and we've designed our infrastructure to protect it with enterprise-grade security measures.

Our core security principles:

  • Complete Encryption: All data is encrypted in transit and at rest using industry-leading standards
  • 100% Customer Ownership: All customer data belongs exclusively to you—we never claim ownership or rights to your information
  • Zero Data Monetization: We never sell, rent, share, or mine your data for any purpose
  • No Training on Your Data: Your data is never used to train AI models or improve our services
  • Private LLM Options: Enterprise customers can deploy private, dedicated LLM instances for maximum security

Data Protection & Encryption

All customer data is encrypted at every stage—from the moment it enters our system until it's delivered to your CRM. We use industry-leading encryption standards to ensure your sensitive business information remains completely secure and inaccessible to unauthorized parties.

EncryptedData in Transit

All communications between Pipeful AI and your CRM are encrypted using TLS 1.3, ensuring your data is protected during transmission. Every API call, data transfer, and connection is secured with end-to-end encryption.

EncryptedData at Rest

Any temporary data we store is encrypted using AES-256 encryption, the same standard used by banks and government agencies. All stored data is encrypted with multiple layers of protection.

OAuth 2.0Authentication

We never store your CRM passwords. All connections use OAuth 2.0, meaning you authenticate directly with Salesforce or HubSpot. Your credentials never pass through our systems.

Minimal DataStorage

We only store the minimum data necessary for functionality. Your CRM data stays in your CRM—we're just the conduit. We never retain your data longer than necessary for service delivery.

Customer Data Ownership & Usage

100% Customer Ownership: All customer data belongs exclusively to you. We are a data processor, not a data owner. You maintain complete ownership and control over all information that flows through Pipeful AI.

Your DataNever Sold or Shared

We will never sell, rent, or share your customer data with third parties. Your CRM data, conversation transcripts, and business information are yours alone and will never be monetized or used for any purpose other than providing you with our service.

No Data MiningNo Training on Your Data

We do not use your customer data to train AI models, improve our algorithms, or for any machine learning purposes. Your data is processed solely for the purpose of delivering the Pipeful AI service to you and is never used to enhance our products or services for other customers.

Complete ControlData Deletion

You can request complete deletion of all your data at any time. Upon request, we will permanently delete all stored data associated with your account within 30 days, with no exceptions or data retention.

TransparentData Processing

We process your data only to provide the Pipeful AI service—connecting your CRM, conducting interviews, and updating records. We do not analyze, aggregate, or use your data for any other business purposes.

Private LLM Deployment

For customers with the highest security and privacy requirements, we offer private LLM deployment options. This ensures your data never touches shared infrastructure or public AI models.

  • Dedicated Infrastructure: Your own isolated LLM instance running on dedicated servers, completely separate from other customers
  • On-Premises Option: Deploy Pipeful AI's LLM capabilities within your own infrastructure for maximum control
  • Private Cloud: Single-tenant deployment in a private cloud environment with your own encryption keys
  • No Shared Resources: Your data is processed exclusively on your dedicated infrastructure, with zero cross-contamination risk
  • Custom Compliance: Private deployments can be configured to meet specific regulatory requirements (HIPAA, FedRAMP, etc.)

Private LLM deployments are available for enterprise customers. Contact our sales team to discuss your specific security requirements and deployment options.

Infrastructure Security

  • Cloud Infrastructure: Hosted on AWS with SOC 2 Type II compliance
  • Regular Audits: Third-party security audits conducted quarterly
  • Access Controls: Role-based access control with multi-factor authentication for our team
  • Monitoring: 24/7 security monitoring and automated threat detection
  • Backup & Recovery: Daily encrypted backups with point-in-time recovery capabilities

Compliance & Certifications

Pipeful AI is committed to meeting the highest standards of data protection and privacy:

  • GDPR Compliant: We comply with the General Data Protection Regulation for EU users
  • CCPA Compliant: California Consumer Privacy Act compliance for California residents
  • SOC 2 Type II: Annual audits ensure our security controls meet industry standards
  • HIPAA Ready: Infrastructure supports HIPAA compliance for healthcare customers (on request)

Your Privacy Rights

You own your data. Period. As the data owner, you have complete control and rights over all information associated with your account:

  • Complete Ownership: All customer data belongs 100% to you. We act only as a processor and never claim ownership or rights to your information
  • Revoke Access: Revoke access to your CRM at any time through your CRM's connected apps settings—immediate and permanent
  • Data Deletion: Request complete deletion of all your data at any time. We will permanently remove all stored data within 30 days
  • Data Export: Export your data in a machine-readable format at any time, with no restrictions or limitations
  • No Secondary Use: Your data is never used for analytics, marketing, research, or any purpose other than delivering the Pipeful AI service
  • Transparency: Request a complete audit of how your data has been processed and stored at any time

Voice Data Handling

When you use Pipeful AI's voice features:

  • Voice recordings are processed in real-time and not stored
  • Only transcribed text is sent to your CRM (never audio files)
  • All voice processing happens through encrypted channels
  • You can disable voice features at any time

Reporting Security Issues

If you discover a security vulnerability, please report it to security@pipeful.ai. We take all reports seriously and will respond within 48 hours. We appreciate responsible disclosure and work with security researchers to keep our platform secure.

Questions About Security?

Our security team is available to answer questions and provide additional documentation for enterprise customers. Contact us to learn more.
